​I am a graduate student in the Robotics Institute at Carnegie Mellon University. I am co-supervised by Prof. Nathan Michael (Robust Adaptive Systems Lab) and Prof. Koushil Sreenath (Hybrid Robotics Lab). I obtained my B.A.Sc. in Engineering Science with Honours from University of Toronto, with a major in Aerospace Engineering and a minor in Robotics and Mechatronics.

My research interests include applying statistical learning techniques to control theory. More recently, my research has focused on online estimation of user intent for control of autonomous mobile robots.
